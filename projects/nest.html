Nest

A second-hand clothing rental & purchase platform

Overview

Nest is a second-hand clothing rental and purchase platform designed for university students. The project originated from our concern about the environmental waste caused by fast fashion and the large number of unused but well-preserved clothes in students’ closets.

Through Nest, we aimed to provide consumers with a quick way to purchase or rent clothing, allow sellers to gain extra income from idle clothes, and offer rental as an alternative for users who are uncertain about size or want to experiment with new styles:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.
